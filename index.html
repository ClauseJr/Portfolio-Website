About Me

I am a results-driven Data Analyst with hands-on experience in transforming complex business problems into clear, actionable insights. My core toolkit includes SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib), enabling me to work across the full data analytics lifecycle — from data extraction, cleaning, and transformation to visualization and insight delivery.

I specialize in designing efficient data workflows, building interactive dashboards, and performing exploratory and diagnostic analysis to uncover trends, patterns, and performance drivers. I have practical experience working with relational databases, writing optimized SQL queries, and developing reports that support business monitoring and decision-making.

I enjoy turning raw, often messy data into meaningful stories that answer not only what is happening, but why it is happening and what actions should be taken next. My focus is on delivering insights that are both technically sound and strategically valuable, helping organizations make confident, data-informed decisions.

I am continuously improving my skills in data analytics and visualization, staying current with modern tools and best practices to deliver high-quality, impactful analytical solutions.
